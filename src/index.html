Who We Are

Welcome at

Max's Tapas Bar

Hoste in Kotor is an amazing place where you can discover great food and hang out with awesome people!

Hoste is a tapas bar opened in June 2017 with food & drinks internationals specialties from Asia to Carribean passing from Europe. Hoste is like a luggage full of souvenirs from different corners of the World, it welcomes you in the UNESCO protected area of Kotor, near the South Gate of the Old Town.

Restaurant

Small savory dishes.

Tapas are a variety of small savory dishes, often served as a snack with drinks, or with other tapas as a meal. It may be cold (such as mixed olives and cheese) or hot (such as chopitos, which are battered, fried baby squid). Tapas have evolved into an entire, sophisticated cuisine. Here in Hoste, we combine various tastes from many cuisines around the world.
